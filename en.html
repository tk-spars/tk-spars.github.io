SPARS Tennis School



Our tennis school has been active for 18 years now. Some of our trainees have become young coaches and hitting partners, others continue their studies abroad and play for their university teams, the rest simply love tennis and play for their pleasure.

Our main goal is to make sure that every one of our pupils finds their place in tennis and has that positive energy which makes us, tennis coaches, love this fascinating sport.

For more detailed information about our tennis school call our head tennis coach Vadim Moroz.
