SPARS Tennis Club



The "SPARS" Tennis Club was founded in 2001. In 2011 all its indoor premises were fully renovated.

The club has two indoor carpet courts and three outdoor clay courts. The indoor courts are available all year round. The outdoor courts are open during the summer season - April to October.

There are several certified tennis coaches working in the club, who specialize both in children group lessons and in individual lessons for people of any level and age. The information about our coaches is available here.

The club regularly holds tournaments for both juniors and adults of professional and amateur level. The photos from our tournaments are available in the photo gallery.

Tennis School



Our tennis school has been active for 15 years now. Some of our trainees have become young coaches and hitting partners, others continue their studies abroad and play for their university teams, the rest simply love tennis and play for their pleasure.

Our main goal is to make sure that every one of our pupils finds their place in tennis and has that positive energy which makes us, tennis coaches, love this fascinating sport.

For more detailed information about our tennis school call our head tennis coach Vadim Moroz.
